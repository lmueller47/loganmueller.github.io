About Me

I am a game designer, developer, creator and collaborator.
I have a passion for creating worlds and experiences that impact those who view my games.

I have been designing games since 2018, learning the ins and outs of Unity, Blender, Visual Studio, and other helpful tools for game developement. I started by journey in high school where I discovered my passion for making games. I have made numerous projects since then that have allowed me to develop and fine tune vital skills in the game design world.

I am ready to continue my Game Design journy at a larger company with a bigger team where I can pursue my passion and get my quality work out to a broader audience.
